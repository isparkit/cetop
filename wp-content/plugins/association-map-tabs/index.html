AITTOP

AITTOP is a member of the Association of Manufacturers and Trading Companies in Fluid Power Transmissions and Components. It is since its 2008 as a class-dominant Spain AITTOP and through its centers AITTOP was founded in 1945 and with the aim of trading technical articles for industry (piping, valves, fittings, pumps, pneumatic transmissions, Photography Technology).


AITTOP is a business where manufacturers, suppliers, distributors, customers and users of transmission and mobile equipment, machine automation, driven by fluid power for hydraulics. The Association advises in numerous projects of standards and regulations in Spain and abroad.
